Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a group of technology enthusiasts who wanted to create a space for innovation, collaboration, and inspiration. What started as a small local meetup has grown into one of the most anticipated global tech conferences, attracting thousands of participants every year.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to empower technologists around the world through knowledge sharing, community building, and celebrating technological advancements. Our mission is to ignite innovation and foster a diverse and inclusive environment where great minds can connect and collaborate.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Maya Johnson

A pioneer in AI ethics, Dr. Johnson has contributed significantly to fair and responsible machine learning practices. Her keynote in 2022 focused on the societal impacts of AI deployment.

Alex Chen

As a leading developer advocate at OpenDev, Alex inspired attendees in 2021 with his insights into open-source ecosystems and community growth.

Lisa Gomez

CEO of FutureTech Labs, Lisa delivered a memorable talk on the future of human-computer interaction and emerging interface technologies at TechCon 2019.
