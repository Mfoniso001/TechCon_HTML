Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for developers passionate about cutting-edge technology. Over the years, it has grown into a global event, attracting thousands of attendees from around the world.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, inclusion, and collaboration in the tech industry. We aim to provide a platform where developers, researchers, and entrepreneurs come together to share ideas and shape the future of technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Linh Tran

Dr. Tran is a leading researcher in artificial intelligence and has pioneered work in ethical machine learning. Her keynote in 2021 challenged the industry to build more transparent systems.

Raj Patel

Raj Patel, CTO at CloudCore, has been instrumental in the evolution of serverless architecture. His 2019 talk set a new benchmark for scalable solutions.

Samantha Lee

Samantha Lee is a cybersecurity expert known for her advocacy in digital rights and online privacy. Her 2022 session emphasized the growing importance of data protection in the digital age.
